Hybrid Model
Hub & Spoke · Operational Focus
  • Combines centralized governance with decentralized execution across departments.
  • Central hub (AI Unit) sets direction; spokes (BI Center, domain teams) execute locally.
  • Balances organizational oversight with local responsiveness and agility.
  • Adopted by large government entities (UAE TDRA, UK GDS, Saudi NDA) for scalable delivery.
  • Prevents capability duplication while preserving domain autonomy and speed.
🏛️
Centre of Excellence (CoE)
Standards & Conformity Focus
  • Specialized central body bringing together expertise, standards, and best practices.
  • Drives organizational conformity through frameworks, policies, and governance structures.
  • Acts as a knowledge hub — enabling teams rather than directly executing delivery.
  • Used in McKinsey, Deloitte, and OECD digital government guidance for AI governance.
  • Ensures alignment with strategic objectives, compliance, and organizational maturity.
Recommended approach: Organizations typically adopt the Hybrid Model as the overarching operating structure, with the AI Unit functioning as a CoE — setting standards and enabling delivery across decentralized BI and analytics teams.
