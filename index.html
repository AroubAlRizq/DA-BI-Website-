Federated Hub & Spoke — Hybrid Model

Operational Focus

Central BI/AI governance combined with domain-embedded analytics teams. Balances enterprise-wide standards with local agility. Adopted by UAE TDRA, Saudi NDA, and UK GDS among others.

  • Central hub sets governance standards; spokes execute locally
  • Prevents duplication while preserving domain autonomy
  • Emphasises how work gets done across decentralised units
  • Balances operational efficiency with responsiveness to local needs
🧩

Centre of Excellence (CoE)

Standardisation Focus

Dedicated AI CoE sets standards, platforms, and frameworks. BI Centers focus on analytics delivery. Used by McKinsey & Deloitte client frameworks and OECD digital government guidance.

  • Centralised body of expertise, best practices, and frameworks
  • Acts as knowledge hub and governance body for the organisation
  • Establishes what should be done and ensures consistency
  • Drives capability building, compliance, and organisational maturity
Key insight: A Hybrid Model may use a CoE as one of its central governance mechanisms — the CoE sets the standards (the "what"), while the Hybrid structure ensures those standards are operationalised across decentralised teams (the "how").

Compare & Contrast:
Hybrid Model vs. CoE
Slide 03
Dimension 🏛️ Hybrid Model 🧩 Centre of Excellence (CoE)
Primary Focus Operational efficiency — balancing central oversight with local execution Standardisation & conformity — embedding best practices across the organisation
Structure Hub-and-spoke: central governance hub with semi-autonomous domain spokes Centralised expert body providing guidance, tools, and frameworks to all teams
Role of Centre Coordinates and governs; spokes retain delivery responsibility Defines standards, builds capability, and ensures organisational alignment
Key Output Agile, locally-relevant analytics and AI solutions delivered at scale Frameworks, policies, reusable platforms, and trained talent pools
Decision Style Distributed — local teams decide within central guardrails Centralised — CoE prescribes the approach and monitors adherence
Relationship These are complementary, not competing. The AI Unit functions as a CoE (governance, standards, enablement), while the BI Center operates as the delivery spoke — together forming a Hybrid Model.
For this organisation: The AI Unit embodies CoE principles (standardisation, governance) while the BI Center embodies Hybrid Model principles (operational delivery). Both co-exist within a unified operating framework.
